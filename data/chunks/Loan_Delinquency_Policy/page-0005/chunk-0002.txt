Expenses of collection shall be charged to “legal and collection” expense.  
 
X.  Share Transfers 
 
Management shall normally be authorized to transfer all shares to the loan when an account is in 
arrears 30 days or more, upon proper notice to the member, prior to the transfer of the account to 
an attorney or collection agency.
