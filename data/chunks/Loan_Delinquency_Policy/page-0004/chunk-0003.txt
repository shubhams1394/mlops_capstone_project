F. Repossession of secured property 
 
1. The credit union will contract with the appropriate local agency, depending on the 
location of the secured property, to repossess the property. 
2. The credit union will arrange for insurance coverage to go into effect on the 
property, using a local agency. 
3. The instructions to the repossession agency will be to develop and send a 
condition report to the credit union within 3 days of the pickup.
