In a cash reserve system, customers must request that the 
bank transfer funds from their preestablished l ine of credit 
to their demand deposit account before negotiating a check 
against them.  A special draft system involves the 
customer negotiating a special check drawn directly 
against a preestablished line of credit.  In that method, 
demand deposit account s are not affected.  In all three 
systems, the bank periodically provides its check credit 
customers with a statement of account activity.  Required 
minimum payments are computed as a fraction of the 
balance of the account on the cycle date and may be made 
by automatic charges to a demand deposit account.
