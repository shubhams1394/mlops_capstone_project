Right to Repu rchase - Some loan participation 
agreements may give the seller a contractual right to 
repurchase the participated interest in the loan at any time.  
In this case, the seller's right to repurchase the 
participation effectively provides the seller with a ca ll 
option on a specific asset and precludes sale accounting.  
If a loan participation agreement contains such a 
provision, the participation should be accounted for as a 
secured borrowing.
