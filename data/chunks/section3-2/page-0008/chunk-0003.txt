In contrast, the vast majority of community banks should 
not be affected by this guidance as they have limited 
involvement in leveraged lending. The agencies do not 
intend that a financial institution that originates a small 
number of less complex, leveraged loans should have 
policies and procedures commensur ate with a larger, more 
complex leveraged loan origination business. However, 
any financial institution that participates in leveraged 
lending transactions should follow applicable supervisory 
guidance provided in the “Participations Purchased” 
section of the guidance.
