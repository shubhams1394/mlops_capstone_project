Credit Risk Rating Assessment and Classification 
Guidelines 
 
The O&G loan policy also should address specific credit 
risk review procedures for the O&G portfolio and O&G 
loan grading criteria.  Risk rating definitions should be 
clearly defined.  RBL that are adequately protected by the 
current sound worth and debt service capaci ty of the 
borrower, guarantor, or underlying collateral generally will 
not be adversely classified for supervisory purposes.

the credit is needed to determine whether the loan has
